You want accounts to do what now?

What started out as, "This customer needs a way to manage their changing teams and view usage," unveiled itself as a need to evolve the "My Account" section across the board. Our customer base and product suite had matured past the current setup, which was still based on single user accounts.

As larger organizations leveraged Whitepages Pro, we saw (and had a few customers vocalize) a need for role stratification and advanced usage info in order to reflect their team's structure and workflow. This impression was further confirmed in face-to-face interviews and observations with customers.
